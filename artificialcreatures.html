SuperSmartSpeedster

The SuperSmartSpeedster bicycle is a state of the art e-bike for young and old alike. Unlike regular e-bikes which just provide cycling assistance the SuperSmartSpeedster will, outside of providing more advanced cycling assistance, also help with your maintenance and safety, both whilst being used or being parked. All of this can be controlled from the control panel on the steering wheel.
The first feature of the SuperSmartSpeedster is the improved cycle assistant. The cycle assistant will dynamically throttle itself in order to achieve a requested safe speed. For this the sensors measure the road around the bike for size and other cyclists, depending if overtake is activated, the SuperSmartSpeedster will accelerate to a point beyond the speed of the cyclist ahead until he or she is overtaken and the bike throttles down again, alternatively the bike will either throttle down to fit perfectly behind the bike in front. This feature can also take slopeage into account and throttle accordingly. If the SuperSmartSpeedster notices a very sudden and abrupt stop the bike will send a message to the control panel to ask its master if it is okay, if this message is not responded to in time, an ambulance will be called.
The second main feature of the SuperSmartSpeedster is its maintenance assistance. Hundreds of tiny sensors are placed all throughout the SuperSmartSpeedster and report back to the control panel. If these sensors notice a malfunction in any of the components, it’s master is notified via a notification on the control panel. The user can then in turn fix the identified issue or make an appointment with the nearest bicycle shop.
is way the SuperSmartSpeedster will travel alongside its master to its place of destination in a quick and safe manner.

Horizon: Zero Dawn

Horizon: Zero Dawn is a game set in the near future after a global catastrophe has thrown humankind back into the iron age, but instead of animals everywhere, giant robots looking like animals roam the land. These robots as it turns out, are controlled by a master program in order to keep the biosphere alive after said catastrophe has wiped out all other living things in the biosphere.
I find this fascinating to see, since it shows delicate food chains being replaced and simulated via robots which react as normal animals do. Herbivores flee for carnivorous machines, even though they don’t actually need to.
This shows that robots could play a role in the future of preserving our biosphere via being literal artificial creatures.

Game created by Guerrilla Games
