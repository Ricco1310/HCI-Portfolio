EDUCATION: VIRTUAL CLASSROOM

Introducing the Virtual classroom, a revolutionary new tool to be used at all levels of education. Using the Virtual classroom teachers and lecturers can effortlessly use one of the many built in environments to demonstrate key concepts to their students. Want to show a secondary grade student how water displacement works, create a real cube of water for you and him to interact with. Want to show your 2nd year student astrophysics the motion of the planets, use the space simulator to accurately and quickly show the motions of the planets and stars. The possibilities are endless with Virtual classroom.

INFRASTRUCTURE: REAL ROAD

Real road is the next big tool for governments to use for managing their already full traffic lanes. Real road project a real-time updating map of the road network of the country on a table for traffic managers in order to steer the traffic of the country in correct ways without too much hassle or oversight. A traffic jam somewhere, the Real road overlay will immediately colour this part red on the highway overview, allowing traffic managers to update their road signs on the fly. Construction work? Real time movement of special guest vehicles. The possibilities are endless with Real Road.

THERAPY: FEAR BE GONE

Fear be gone is a new therapy developed by therapist using confrontation therapy in a VR environment. It is exceedingly good at confronting people with conflict situation where the user is at the mercy of VR, whilst still being totally safe and near a medical professional. Fear be gone has been developed for usage with treating PTSD situations, by recreating the environment where the traumatic experience took place. This way people can relive the situation whilst being helped to come to terms with it by a medical professional.

Research

Fove

Fove is a revolutionary new VR headset which utilizes the most up to date sensors, which are mounted within the Fove itself, to get accurate readings of your eyes and where they are looking. Furthermore it also dynamically changes the quality of the screens based on where you are looking so that even less powerful pc’s can run the Fove. Fove allows the user to control the VR environment with their eyes and is the next step in VR technologies, since it is so huge to be able to control things by looking at them.



Read on https://www.wareable.com/vr/fove-review.

Ikea Place

Ikea place offers a method for potential buyers of new Ikea furniture to have a look using an AR app how certain pieces of furniture would look inside of their own house. Ikea Place allows user to pick from a variety of furniture options and move and see them in real time within the house. This application is run completely on the user’s phone. This shows a quick and easy way to display large amounts of information to the user.



Found at https://www.onemorething.nl/2017/09/ikea-place-augmented-reality-beschikbaar/
