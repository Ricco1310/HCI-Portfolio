Unity

The enervating and expansive area around the player allows for a freedom om movement unparalleled anywhere else, furthermore two historically accurate models of Russian artillery tanks are placed within the world for the player character to interact with. The player can watch as one of the artillery tanks catches fire upon contact, whilst the other one explodes. Lastly a ball is placed within the environment to play with. This makes the player feel like they truly can interact with and experience authentic wartime vehicles.
